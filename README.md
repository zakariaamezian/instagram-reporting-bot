## 📋 What is instagram-reporting-bot?

instagram-reporting-bot is a tool that helps you report Instagram accounts that might break Instagram’s rules. It works by sending reports automatically. You can use it to report fake profiles, spam accounts, or any other accounts you believe violate Instagram’s policies. The bot supports sending multiple reports from different accounts to increase the chance of action on the reported profile.

This tool is designed to handle many reports at once, making your reporting faster and easier.

## ⚙️ How to Use instagram-reporting-bot

The bot works by sending report requests through Instagram’s report system. Here is how you use it:

1. Prepare one or multiple Instagram accounts to report.  
2. Enter the usernames you want to report.  
3. Choose the reason for reporting, like spam or fake profile.  
4. Start the bot. It will send reports automatically from the accounts you use.

The bot runs until it finishes sending all reports or you stop it.
